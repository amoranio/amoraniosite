QuickGroup Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2026

SUMMARY
QuickGroup does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data stays on your device.

DATA COLLECTION
QuickGroup does not collect any data. Specifically:
- No personal information is collected
- No browsing history is collected
- No tab URLs or titles are collected
- No analytics or tracking is used
- No data is sent to external servers

LOCAL STORAGE
QuickGroup stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:
- Group state (whether tabs are currently grouped)
- User preference for "Keep current tab visible" setting

This data never leaves your browser and is only used to maintain functionality between sessions.

PERMISSIONS USED
- tabs: Required to group and ungroup your tabs
- tabGroups: Required to create, collapse, and manage tab groups
- storage: Required to remember your preferences and group state

These permissions are used solely for the core functionality of the extension. No data accessed through these permissions is collected or transmitted.

THIRD PARTIES
QuickGroup does not share any data with third parties because no data is collected.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in the extension's store listing.

CONTACT
For questions about this privacy policy, visit: https://amoran.io
